Bio

Hi there! I'm a second-year Ph.D. student at CS department of Rice University working with Prof. Ang Chen. I got my BS degree from Beijing University of Posts and Telecommunications (BUPT) in 2019. I am broadly interested in systems, networking, and security, with a particular focus on the intersection of programmable networks with machine learning and program analysis.
